SECOND BALTIC YOUNG SCIENTISTS’ INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL ON “MAGNETOSPHERE-IONOSPHERE-ATMOSPHERE COUPLING”

Second Baltic School “MIAC”

05 - 09 June, 2018. Zelenogradsk, Kaliningrad Region, Russia

About The Conference

Second Baltic School “MIAC” will bring together young scientists, postgraduates, and undergraduates under the age of 39.

Main topics of Baltic School “MIAC”:

- Atmosphere-ionosphere coupling

- Solar-terrestrial physics, space climate and space whether

- Global electric circuit and magnetosphere-ionosphere current system

- Global satellite positioning system disruption during solar flares and geomagnetic storms

- Ionospheric plasma turbulence and irregularities

- Wave-particle interaction in magnetosphere
